* ======= Swap the bracket and curly brace keys =====
* This mod makes it nicer to work with programming
* languages that use lots of curly {} braces.

* Copy this into your layout's .txt file on your Kinesis Advantage 2:
{obrack}>{speed9}{-lshift}{obrack}{+lshift}
{lshift}{obrack}>{speed9}{obrack}
{rshift}{obrack}>{speed9}{obrack}
{cbrack}>{speed9}{-lshift}{cbrack}{+lshift}
{lshift}{cbrack}>{speed9}{cbrack}
{rshift}{cbrack}>{speed9}{cbrack}
