* ======= Swap the bracket and curly brace keys =====
* This mod makes it nicer to work with programming
* languages that use lots of curly {} braces.
{obrack}>{-lshift}{obrack}{+lshift}
{cbrack}>{-lshift}{cbrack}{+lshift}
{lshift}{obrack}>{obrack}
{lshift}{cbrack}>{cbrack}
{rshift}{cbrack}>{cbrack}
{rshift}{obrack}>{obrack}
