* ----- Simple WASD Remap by shifting each letter over one position -----
* ROW 1: WERT
[W]>[Q]
[E]>[W]
[R]>[E]
[T]>[R]

* ROW 2: SDFG
[S]>[A]
[D]>[S]
[F]>[D]
[G]>[F]

* ----- Swap SPACE and BACKSPACE -----
* Many games use SPACE for movements such as JUMP...
[bspace]>[space]
[space]>[bspace]
